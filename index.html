About Me

Hi, welcome to my portfolio site! I'm Vanessa, a software engineer with experience working with web dev, game dev, and XR projects. My favorite technologies for web development are React.js, Node.js, Django (Python), SASS, and Postgres. For game development, I am most experienced in Unity and C#.
